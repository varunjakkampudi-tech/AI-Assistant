### Long description (Play Store / App Store, max 4000 chars)

ORA OS is your personal AI operating system — a cinematic, dark-glass companion that quietly runs the background of your life so you can focus on the foreground.

**One Ask, anywhere.** Ask ORA anything by voice or text. Powered by Amazon Bedrock (Nova Lite) and ElevenLabs cloned voice, it talks back in a warm, natural tone — and remembers what matters.

**Your day, summarized.** Open ORA each morning and get a single, beautiful briefing: weather, calendar, inbox, missed calls, top priorities, and an AI focus score.

**Finance Brain.** Connect Gmail and ORA auto-detects every bank, UPI and credit-card transaction. See spending trends, top categories, recurring drains, and personalized insights — without spreadsheets.

**Health on autopilot.** Log sleep, steps, workouts, water, weight, mood. ORA spots trends, celebrates streaks, and nudges you when something drifts.

**Career Copilot.** Paste a job URL — ORA scores it against your resume, generates a tailored resume + cover letter + interview prep kit, and tracks your application pipeline.

**A life Timeline.** Every chat, email, meeting and transaction becomes a beautifully filtered timeline of your day. Scroll back. Travel forward.

**Memory that compounds.** People you mention, projects you're working on, dates that matter — ORA quietly saves them, then surfaces them when relevant. Your second brain, finally.

**Plus:** AI Journal, Chief of Staff, Knowledge Vault (RAG over your documents), Digital Twin (reply-in-your-voice drafts), Knowledge Graph, AI Calls.

**Design that breathes.** Antique gold on deep black. Generous space. Real glass. Built to feel expensive, not corporate. Phosphor-style icons. Cinematic motion.

**Privacy first.** Your data stays in your private vault. Google connect is optional. Bank notifications never leave your device unless you say so.

**Built on:** Amazon Bedrock (Nova Lite), ElevenLabs cloned voice, OpenAI Whisper transcription, FastAPI backend, MongoDB.

This is v1. Many of the features above already work end-to-end; some integrations (telephony, real bank APIs) are intentionally mocked so the experience is testable today. We're shipping fast, listening hard.

— Made with care. Welcome to ORA OS.
